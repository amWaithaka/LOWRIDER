Craft service, often referred to as "crafty," is an essential aspect of the film industry, particularly on movie sets. Here's an overview:

Responsibilities

Craft service professionals are responsible for providing snacks, beverages, and sometimes light meals to all crew members throughout the production process [2].

Availability

Craft services are typically available for the entire duration of the film shoot, ensuring that crew members have access to refreshments and sustenance whenever needed [3].

Importance

Craft services play a crucial role in maintaining the well-being and morale of the crew, as long hours on set can be physically and mentally demanding [6].

Role on Set

Craft service workers are integral to the smooth functioning of a film set, catering to the needs of everyone from production assistants to makeup artists and cast members [6].

Training and Qualifications

While formal qualifications are not always required, individuals entering the craft service profession should possess good organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work efficiently under pressure [5].

Craft service ensures that crew members remain energized and focused during long hours of filming, contributing to the overall success of the production.
